# Game Design Notes

## Vertical Slice Goal

Build one polished mission that proves the full game loop:

1. Jeremie detects an activated tower.
2. The player enters Lyoko in the Forest Sector.
3. The player swaps between Odd, Ulrich and Yumi depending on combat need.
4. Aelita follows as a protected companion.
5. The team crosses the sector using overvehicles.
6. X.A.N.A. sends mixed enemy groups.
7. Aelita reaches the tower and enters `CODE: LYOKO`.
8. Mission complete, with a short operator-style debrief.

## Character Pillars

- Odd: speed, ranged pressure, aerial style, Overboard tricks.
- Ulrich: close combat, katana burst damage, dash/clones, Overbike ramming.
- Yumi: control, fan throws, telekinetic pulls, stable Overwing movement.
- Aelita: objective character, shield support, tower interaction, puzzle utility.

## Enemy Pillars

- Kankrelat: small swarms that teach target switching.
- Blok: turret monster that forces dodge timing.
- Krab: heavier pressure enemy with visible weak point.
- Hornet: flying harassment enemy that rewards lock-on and ranged attacks.
- Megatank: mini-boss that teaches waiting for the armor/eye opening.

## First Mission Beat Sheet

- Spawn on the first Forest Sector plateau.
- Jeremie warns that the tower is active and X.A.N.A. has isolated the path.
- Two Kankrelats test basic attack.
- The bridge introduces vehicle traversal over the Digital Sea.
- Blok and Krab guard the middle platform.
- Hornet appears once the player nears the tower plateau.
- Megatank blocks the tower entrance.
- Aelita must stand close to the tower while the player holds `E`.
- Final text: tower cleared, return program ready.
